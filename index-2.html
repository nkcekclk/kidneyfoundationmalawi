Who We Are

Girls Can Malawi is an initiative dedicated to empowering girls through access to quality education and life-changing opportunities.

We believe that:

Girls can break the cycle of poverty

Girls can contribute to Malawi's development

Girls can achieve better health outcomes

Girls can enjoy equality and dignity

Our Commitment to Sustainable Development

Aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, we're working towards gender equality and quality education for all.

Why Girls?

Girls remain one of the most vulnerable groups in society. They face discrimination in education, health, employment, and decision-making spaces.

By prioritizing girls, we aim to:

Correct gender inequalities

Challenge harmful beliefs and stereotypes

Promote equal opportunities

Empower girls to become leaders
