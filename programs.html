Our Key Initiatives

1

Prevention

Prevention Program

We work toward helping people at risk to take the steps they need to prevent or slow the progression of kidney diseases. We promote the effective life style to deter people from having the problem. This is done through the radio programs and public meetings.

What We Provide:

  • Community education on kidney disease prevention and early warning signs
  • Promotion of healthy lifestyles to reduce the risk of kidney disease and NCDs
  • Radio and television programs on kidney health and prevention
  • Community outreach through public meetings and health talks
  • Awareness campaigns on reducing harmful practices linked to kidney disease

2

Education

Education Program

With the help of the medical personnel and using our lived experience we offer education using the public and private radio and Television to give the general public the required information. Every year we participate the World Kidney Day as one ware of doing the awareness. .

Our Support Includes:

  • Public education on kidney diseases through radio and television programs
  • Awareness campaigns during World Kidney Day and other health observances
  • Information sharing based on lived experiences of people with kidney disease
  • Community sensitization on prevention, early diagnosis, and treatment options
  • Educational materials on kidney health and NCD prevention
Support This Program
3

Advocacy

Advocacy Program

We take a leadership role in advocating for policies and programs to meet the needs of people living with kidney diseases and those at risk. We are using our lived experience as a tool for the advocacy

Our Support Includes:

  • Advocacy for policies that improve prevention, treatment, and care for kidney diseases
  • Engagement with government, policymakers, and stakeholders using lived experiences
  • Representation of people living with kidney diseases in national health discussions
  • Advocacy for inclusion of kidney diseases within NCD strategies and policies
  • Collaboration with civil society organizations to strengthen advocacy efforts
Support This Program
4

Support Renal Services in Malawi

Support Renal Services in Malawi Program

We support to improve the renal service in Malawi like coordinating with other stakeholders like ISN to train the medical personnel and make sure that the quality of dialysis being given to patients is of friendly and international standard.

Our Support Includes:

  • Support for improving the quality of renal and dialysis services in Malawi
  • Coordination with partners such as ISN and other stakeholders to train medical personnel
  • Advocacy for patient-friendly and internationally accepted dialysis standards
  • Capacity building for healthcare workers involved in renal care
  • Support for improving access to renal services for patients across the country
Support This Program
5

Being a Member of NCD Alliance

Being a Member of NCD Alliance

As a member we corroborate with other NCD groups and organization to amplify the problems and challenges of People Living with NCDs in Malawi and participate in the activities of Malawi NCD Alliance.

Our Support Includes:

  • Collaboration with other NCD organizations to amplify the voices of people living with NCDs
  • Participation in Malawi NCD Alliance activities, campaigns, and consultations
  • Joint advocacy on shared challenges affecting people living with NCDs
  • Knowledge sharing and collective learning among NCD stakeholders
  • Strengthening national efforts to address NCDs, including kidney diseases
